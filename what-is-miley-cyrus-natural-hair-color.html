    Miley Cyrus is a multi-talented former child star turned international pop singer, songwriter, and actor. The star keeps her look fresh by changing her style and hair color from time to time.

    Miley Cyrus’ is a natural brunette with some hints of caramel. This can be easily observed comparing her hair color from the days of her hit TV show “Hannah Montana” to her look today. 

    Miley dyes her hair to achieve the look for which she is most synonymous – a cross between blonde and ombre. 

    Break-out Star 

    Miley Ray Cyrus is the famous child of her equally famous father, country music singer Billy Ray Cyrus.

    She first gained favor with audiences as a precocious fresh-faced brunette while playing the title character in the Disney Channel production of Hannah Montana.

    The show premiered in 2006 and ran for 5 years. At the time, Disney Channel received the highest ratings in history upon the show’s debut. 

    Hannah Montana showcased both Miley Cyrus’ acting abilities and singing chops over the course of the show. It was through Disney’s media franchise that she scored two number 1 hits on the Billboard Top 200 charts. 

    Miley Cyrus sported her own natural hair – a beautiful brown with hints of caramel – throughout her run on the show. Her hairstyles were typically those of any teenager.

    At certain points, Miley’s character donned a blonde wig to portray her alter ego – a pop singer at night – on the show.

    Changing It Up

    Miley Cyrus’ hair has run the gamut of color and length for as long as she has been in the spotlight. 

    It ranges from short and blonde to a mohawk, to long with extensions and platinum blonde. 

    In her performances or while on tour overseas, Miley has also sported multiple buns, a shocking rainbow style, and sometimes wears wigs.  

    Hair As Self-Expression

    Following Miley Cyrus’ break-up with her husband, Liam, in 2019, she posted a series of photographs on her Instagram page, shared with her 111 million followers.  

    In a few of the photos, fans couldn’t help but notice that her hair was now a bright bleach blonde, one of her brightest blonde looks to date. 

    This led fans to speculate that the new color was an expression of Miley celebrating her break up. 

    Denying that this look was part of her post-breakup makeover, Miley indicated that this was simply an affirmation of her love for bleach and that she was getting ready for Coachella.
