    Swedish professional Ice Hockey player Carl Hagelin plays for NHL team Washington Capitals. Besides his soaring NHL career, he won two silver in the 2008 World Junior Ice Hockey Championships and 2014 Winter Olympics representing Sweden.

    Carl Hagelin Bio & Family

    The player was born as Carl Oliver Hagelin on 23 August 1988 in Nykvarn, Sweden. Talking about his family, he has a brother, Bobbie Halegin and a sister named Halene Hagelin. Apart from the two siblings, the details of their parents are still missing.

    Carl stands at the height of 5 feet 11 inches (180 cm) and weighs around 84 kg.

    Career & Net Worth

    The 30-year-old college career commenced playing for Södertälje SK‘s team in the J20 SuperElit. In the club, his incredible stats were 44 scored goals including 51 assists. After that, he got the fortune for playing college hockey for the University of Michigan Wolverines, ice hockey team and led the squad to be victorious at the CCHA championship.

    Some years later, the New York Rangers picked him in the sixth round, 168th overall at the 2007 NHL Entry Draft. However, his NHL debut came against the Washington Capitals on 25 November 2011. During the play, he made an assist on a goal by Brian Boyle for his first NHL points. Almost four years playing with Rangers, he was traded to Anaheim Ducks on 27 June 2015 in exchange for Emerson Etem. Likewise, he penned a four-year contract with Ducks worth shinning millions on 14 August 2015. Nevertheless, he went to Pittsburgh Penguins and played the 2015/2016 and 2016/2017 season before Penguins traded him to the Los Angeles Kings in exchange for Tanner Pearson on 14 November 2018.

    Carl played only 22 games with Lakers and then moved to Capitals as a trade exchange on 21 February 2019.

    As if for now, Carl has a net worth of $2 million playing for five different NHL teams.

    Carl & Wife’s Baby Post One Year of Wedding

    The Capitals star and his wife, Erica Uebel were in the long term committed relationship before sharing their wedding vows on 16 July 2017. Almost a year after the wedding, the family became three upon the arrival of a baby daughter, Blanche on 29 April 2019.

    Carl Hagelin and his wife Erica Uebel with their daughter Blanche on 23 June 2018 (Picture: Carl’s Instagram)

    Carl loves to spend most of his free time with his wife and in the company of their newly-born.

    Some Interesting Facts About Carl Hagelin

    • Carl’s brother, Bobbie Hagelin, born on 1 February 1984 in Södertälje, Sweden is a retired ice hockey player. His former team is the Södertälje SK in the Swedish Elite League and for Rødovre Mighty Bulls in the Danish AL-Bank Ligaen.
    • The player is the grandson of Boris Hagelin, a former late Swedish business tycoon. His grandfather (b. 2 July 1892 – 7 September 1983) was an inventor of encryption machines.
    • Carl is the first Swedish player to play for Central Collegiate Hockey Association (CCHA) at the University level.
