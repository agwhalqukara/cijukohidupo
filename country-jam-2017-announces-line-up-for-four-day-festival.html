    Country Jam 2017 announces line-up for four day festival

    Country Jam has revealed its 2017 line up, with some huge country stars set to perform. The four-day festival takes place at Jam Ranch, Grand Junction in Colorado, and is now in its twenty-sixth year.

    08 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Country Jam has revealed its 2017 line up, with some huge country stars set to perform. The four-day festival takes place at Jam Ranch, Grand Junction in Colorado, and is now in its twenty-sixth year.

    Country Jam took to their Twitter page earlier today to share the news.

    Here's the #CountryJamCO 2017 daily lineup! Which day are you most excited for? You can also buy single day tickets: https://t.co/TZJ96bFM65 pic.twitter.com/ZZIVqU2Y1m

    — Country Jam Colorado (@CountryJamCO) April 19, 2017

    Justin Moore and Randy Houser will be co-headlining on the opening night. Jason Aldean who recently achieved his nineteenth number one single and won an ACM award for ‘Entertainer of The Year’, will also be headlining, on Friday night. As well as this, Kenny Chesney will be the headliner for Saturday. He has recently had a number one with his latest album Cosmic Hallelujah. Rounding the festival off, Thomas Rhett will be headlining on Sunday. He recently won an ACM award for ‘Male Vocalist of The Year’.

    The festival is becoming one of the biggest for country music fans, after drawing in 90,000 fans last year. With this year’s amazing line-up it is sure to be one massive party from start to finish.

    The festival is due to take place from August 15th-19th.

    There is a range of tickets available from general admission to VIP. For information on tickets, you can visit the festival’s website here.
