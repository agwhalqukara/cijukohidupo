    Did Katy Perry just chop off all her hair?  

    The “This Is How We Do” singer, 30, is no stranger to wild hairstyle colors (she’s tried pink, green, and just about the whole rainbow), so we wouldn’t put it past her to play with length.  

    On Wednesday, April 1, Perry posted a photo to Instagram of herself sporting an edgy black pixie. At first, the caption read: “Chop Chop.” But she quickly edited the text to, “I asked for the Kris Jenner.“

    Because it is April Fools’ Day, it’s safe to take the star’s latest hair makeover with a grain of salt. 

    Just two days before, Perry and Jenner hung out on a yacht at Karl Lagerfeld‘s dinner for Chanel, along with Kendall Jenner, Gigi Hadid, and Dakota Johnson.
