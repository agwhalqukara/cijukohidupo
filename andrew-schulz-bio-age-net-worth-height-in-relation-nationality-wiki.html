Andrew has a brother named Greg. Moreover, he has German and Irish ancestry on his father’s side of the family and has Scottish ancestry on his mother’s side.

    Professional Life

    Andrew Schulz is most famous for being on popular shows such as Guy Code, Jobs That Don’t Suck, and Sneaky Pete. He even regularly has his own stand-up comedy show and hosts a podcast with Akaash Singh called Flagrant 2, which is on Soundcloud and YouTube.

    He is known for his work on Guy Code, Flagrant 2 podcast, The Brilliant Idiots podcast, and the Amazon original series Sneaky Pete. Moreover, in December 2020, Netflix announced it received a Schulz special.

    Stand up comedy

    In September 2017, Andrew self-released his first comedy special, “4:4:1”. His special aired on YouTube. Similarly, in June 2018, he followed up with his debut comedy album, “5:5:1”. Moreover, in the album’s first week of release, it earned number one ranking on the iTunes comedy album charts, Apple Music, Google Play, and Amazon. Furthermore, for the week of June 23, 2018, the album was number one on the Billboard comedy album charts.
