    RJ And Luna And Bill And Poppy Land At The Beach House Retreat, Awkward Encounter

    The Bold and the Beautiful (B&amp;B) spoilers tease that RJ Forrester (Joshua Hoffman) and Luna Nozawa (Lisa Yamada) are growing closer by the hour, notwithstanding his cousin Zende Forresters (Delon de Metz) interference.

    29 Aug 2024, 00:00

    The Bold and the Beautiful (B&B) spoilers tease that RJ Forrester (Joshua Hoffman) and Luna Nozawa (Lisa Yamada) are growing closer by the hour, notwithstanding his cousin Zende Forrester’s (Delon de Metz) interference.

    During the week of January 15-19 Luna and RJ make a big step to take their relationship to the next level.

    The Bold And The Beautiful Spoilers: RJ And Luna Commit In Their Relationship

    If RJ and Luna are going to sleep together they need to find a suitable place where they can be alone and undisturbed. RJ has the answer–the new beach house he rented.

    Only problem is, this could be the very same beach house that Taylor Hayes (Krista Allen) bought back from Wyatt Spencer (Darin Brooks) in 2022 in order to surprise Ridge Forrester (Thorsten Kaye).

    Taylor is off licking her wounds after having lost Ridge Forrester (Thorsten Kaye) to Brooke Logan once again. Maybe she rented the place to RJ in her absence?

    If so, RJ will be stunned and interrupted when he takes Luna there on January 19. B&amp;B spoilers tease that Luna and RJ encounter an astonishing sight!

    B&B Spoilers: RJ’s Surprise At The Beach House

    Which brings us to Bill Spencer (Don Diamont) and Poppy Nozawa’s (Romy Park) second date. These are two consenting adults who have been around the block and need no excuse to enjoy some alone time together.

    Could it be that Bill thinks the beach house still belongs to Wyatt and takes Poppy there on the spur of the moment? However this works out, two couples wind up at the cozy cottage—Bill and Poppy and Luna and RJ. Awkward!

    The Bold And The Beautiful Spoilers: Bill’s Second Date With Poppy Goes Awry

    They may be half clothed and inebriated (or not) but it looks like Bill, Poppy, Luna, and RJ encounter some comical confusion as they try to figure out how each couple came to be in the same place at the same time.

    Luna will be embarrassed because Poppy is her mom, but are we also going to find out sooner or later that Bill is her dad?!
