    Lincoln's a big sister! Kristen Bell and Dax Shepard have welcomed their second baby girl together. The couple announced the news via Twitter on Friday, Dec. 19.

    "Delta Bell Shepard is here. She doesn't look like either ?@IMKristenBell? or me, but we're gonna keep her anyway. ?#christmascameearly?" Shepard tweeted. 

    The proud mom also tweeted, "The estrogen has now hit critical mass! Good luck, @daxshepard1.  And welcome to the world DELTA BELL SHEPARD :)"

    Us confirmed in June that Bell, 34, and the Parenthood actor, 39, were expecting again. "The whole family is beyond excited," a rep for the couple told Us at the time.

    During her "really easy" pregnancy, Bell gave Us new insight about daughter Lincoln, 20 months. "She can sign now and she knows 40-50 signs! I can talk to her and she'll sign in three, four word sentences which is really amazing," the Veronica Mars alum told Us in September. "She's the smartest baby ever!" she joked.
