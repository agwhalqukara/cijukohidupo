    Halsey Loses It On Fan Who Brings Up G Eazys Name During Her Performance

    Halsey is heavily promoting her new album Manic. The pop star recently performed at a show where she went off on a heckler. Halsey was singing at her show during star-studded Miami Super Bowl Weekend when an annoying audience member repeatedly yelled her exs name.

    18 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Halsey is heavily promoting her new album Manic. The pop star recently performed at a show where she went off on a heckler.

    Halsey was singing at her show during star-studded Miami Super Bowl Weekend when an annoying audience member repeatedly yelled her ex’s name.

    Mid-performance, Halsey confronted the crowd in a video that has since gone viral.

    ‘If you say G-Eazy one more f***** time, I will kick you out this building. I will kick your f***** a**, test me. I will kick your ass out the f****** club. You’re not going to disrespect me like that at my own show.’

    She couldn’t point out the exact person who was responsible but then led the crowd on a ‘f*** that guy’ chant.

    She then addressed the incident in her Instagram Story.

    https://twitter.com/yvritza/status/1223847543647481861?

    ‘Don’t ever let someone make you feel crazy or unhinged cause ur a woman standing up for yourself,” she wrote. “Don’t tolerate disrespect in the name of being ‘nice.’ Love u.’

    Halsey dated the rapper on and off in 2017. She’s known for discussing her breakups through her music and even dissed him for cheating during a performance on Saturday Night Live.

    The Colors singer is now in a relationship with American Horror Story actor Evan Peters. She recently revealed that she won’t be dating any more musicians anytime soon.

    ‘I stopped dating musicians which is for my own benefit. I want everyone to be the best version of themselves so much that I sometimes don’t focus on making me the best version of myself. I do everything in my power to help them succeed… And then, at some point, I’m like where do I fit in this box.’
